*Initial 3-Month Training (Common for All)*

1. *Introduction to Computing*
   - Resource: [Computer Science 101](https://online.stanford.edu/courses/soe-ycscs101-computer-science-101)

2. *Version Control (Git)*
   - Resource: [Pro Git Book](https://git-scm.com/book/en/v2)

3. *Programming Language (Python)*
   - Resource: [Python for Everybody](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/python)

4. *Data Structures and Algorithms*
   - Resource: [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/)

5. *Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) Concepts*
   - Resource: [Python OOP Tutorial](https://realpython.com/python3-object-oriented-programming/)

6. *Error Handling*
   - Resource: [Python Exceptions](https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/errors.html)

7. *Typing Speed*
   - Resource: [TypingClub](https://www.typingclub.com/)

8. *Problem Solving*
   - Resource: [HackerRank](https://www.hackerrank.com/)

*Specialization Paths*

After the initial training, team members can choose a specialization:

*1. Backend Development*

   - *Django*
     - Resource: [Django Official Documentation](https://docs.djangoproject.com/en/stable/)
   - *Databases*
     - Resource: [Database Systems Concepts](https://www.coursera.org/learn/database-systems)
   - *Firebase*
     - Resource: [Firebase Documentation](https://firebase.google.com/docs)
   - *FastAPI*
     - Resource: [FastAPI Tutorial](https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/tutorial/)
   - *Plugins*
     - Resource: [Writing Plugins](https://realpython.com/python-plugins/)
   - *Security*
     - Resource: [OWASP Top Ten](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/)
   - *Pipeline Design*
     - Resource: [CI/CD Pipeline](https://www.redhat.com/en/topics/devops/what-is-ci-cd)

*2. Software Engineering*

   - *Requirement Gathering*
     - Resource: [Requirements Engineering](https://www.coursera.org/learn/requirements-engineering)

   - *Models (Waterfall, Agile)*
     - Resource: [Agile vs. Waterfall](https://www.atlassian.com/agile/project-management/project-management-methodologies)

*3. Frontend Development*

   - *HTML & CSS*
     - Resource: [MDN Web Docs](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web)
   - *Streamlit*
     - Resource: [Streamlit Documentation](https://docs.streamlit.io/)
   - *XML*
     - Resource: [XML Tutorial](https://www.w3schools.com/xml/)

     - Resource: [Interaction Design Foundation](https://www.interaction-design.org/)

     - Resource: [React Official Documentation](https://reactjs.org/docs/getting-started.html)

*4. Cybersecurity*

This structured guide provides a clear pathway for new team members to follow, ensuring a consistent and comprehensive onboarding experience.
